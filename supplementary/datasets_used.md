# Datasets Used

| Dataset | Description | Temporal Coverage | Spatial Resolution | Source |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| VIIRS FIRMS Active Fire | Nighttime VIIRS 375m active fire detections, filtered to non-low confidence and scan/track ≤ 0.5 | 2023–2025 | 375 m | NASA FIRMS (VNP14IMGTDL_NRT / VJ114IMGTDL_NRT) |
| Historical BC Fire Perimeters 2023/2024 | Final fire perimeter polygons for the 2023 fire season in the Fort St. John and Fort Nelson Fire Zones | 2023 | Vector | BC Wildfire Service, BC Data Catalogue |
| Current BC Fire Perimeters (2025) | Final fire perimeter polygons for the 2025 fire season | 2025 | Vector | BC Wildfire Service, BC Data Catalogue |
| MODIS Snow Disappearance Date 2024/2025 | Per-pixel day of year of snow disappearance derived from MODIS Terra/Aqua | 2024 | 500 m | MODIS MOD10A1 / MYD10A1, processed to annual SDD raster following SnowCloudMetrics Crumley et al. (2020) |
| CG Lightning Flash Data 2024/2025 | 3-hourly gridded cloud-to-ground lightning flash counts at 0.1-degree resolution | 2024 | 0.1° (~11 km) | Environment and Climate Change Canada, Canadian Lightning Detection Network (CLDN) |
| Road Network | Road centreline network for the Fort St. John and Fort Nelson Fire Zones, with road class attribute (RD_CLASS) | Current | Vector | BC Data Catalogue, Digital Road Atlas (DRA) |
